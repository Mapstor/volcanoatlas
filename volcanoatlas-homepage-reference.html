About VolcanoAtlas

VolcanoAtlas is the comprehensive online encyclopedia dedicated to Earth's volcanoes. Our mission is to make volcanic science accessible through detailed profiles, interactive maps, and data visualizations.

Each volcano profile includes eruption histories, geological data, hazard assessments, and visitor information. Our data is sourced from the Smithsonian Institution's Global Volcanism Program, ensuring scientific accuracy.
